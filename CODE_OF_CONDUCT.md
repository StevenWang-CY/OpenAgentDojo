# Code of Conduct

OpenAgentDojo adopts the
[Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)
as its code of conduct. The full text is canonical and lives at the link
above; this file restates the headline commitments and the local
enforcement path.

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in the OpenAgentDojo community a
welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of background. We commit
to acting in ways that contribute to an open, friendly, and healthy
community.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that contribute to a positive environment:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other contributors.
- Being respectful of differing opinions and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Taking responsibility, apologising, and learning from mistakes.
- Focusing on what is best for the overall community.

Examples of unacceptable behaviour are enumerated in the upstream
[Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
The shorthand: do not harass, do not target individuals, and do not
share other people's private information without consent.

## Scope

This code applies in all project spaces — the GitHub repository, issue
tracker, pull-request discussions, any project chat — and in any space
where you represent the project publicly.

## Enforcement

Report incidents to the maintainers at **`conduct@openagentdojo.app`**.
Reports are treated confidentially. Maintainers investigate every report
and respond in a way they judge appropriate to the circumstances, up to
and including a temporary or permanent ban from project spaces.
